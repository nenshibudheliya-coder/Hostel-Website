📋 Rules and Discipline

Hostel Rules

🕙
Curfew Timing

All students must return to the hostel by 10:00 PM. Prior permission from the warden is required for late entry.

🚫
No Boys Entry

Male entry is strictly prohibited on the hostel premises. Visitors are only permitted up to the designated reception area.

🍃
No Smoking / Alcohol

Smoking, alcohol consumption, and any kind of intoxication are strictly prohibited in the hostel at all times.

🔇
Silence Hours

A silence zone must be maintained from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM. Loud music or excessive noise is completely forbidden.

🧹
Cleanliness

Always keep your room and common areas neat and clean. It is everyone's responsibility to contribute to the cleanliness of the hostel.

📝
Leave Permission

It is mandatory to inform the Warden in advance before going out. Valid approval from parents is also strictly required.
